# Project PANOPTES

**Enabling a worldwide network of robotic telescopes to detect transiting exoplanets.**

Project PANOPTES (Panoptic Astronomical Networked Observatories for a Public Transversal Evolutionary Survey) is an open-source citizen science project. Our goal is to democratize astronomy by providing the software and hardware designs necessary for anyone to build and operate their own automated observatory.

## 3. Get Involved

Whether you are an astronomer, a software developer, or a citizen scientist, there is a place for you in the network.

* **Build a Unit:** Check out our hardware build guides to start your own observatory.
* **Contribute Code:** Help us decouple our hardware layers and improve our TUI interfaces.
* **Join the Discussion:** Visit the [PANOPTES Forum](https://forum.projectpanoptes.org) to meet the community.
